About US

Forecast For All !

Our brand mission is to make quality weather and pollution information available to every person on this planet. No matter where you live in the world or how obscure an activity you require weather information for - we will provide you with as much relevant, local weather data as we can uncover. We promise to provide weather data for those that are underserved by other weather providers.

Data as Design Especially in a world where so much data is consumed on the small screen of a mobile phone, we have invested a lot of time and energy into the design of our products across all digital platforms. We have created a user experience that enables you to find as much weather data as you need as quickly as possible.
